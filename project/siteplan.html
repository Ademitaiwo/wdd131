Portfolio Website Plan

Site Name

My Portfolio - This site will showcase my personal projects, skills, and achievements. I chose this name because it is simple, clear, and represents me professionally.

Optional domain: myportfolio.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this portfolio website is to display my work in web design, programming, and graphic design. It will also provide a way for visitors to contact me for collaborations or job opportunities.

Planned Site Content

The planned website will be a personal portfolio site designed to showcase my work, skills, and professional background. The site will include the following sections:

  • Home Page: An introduction and a brief personal statement.
  • About Me: Background information, education, and career interests.
  • Projects/Works: A gallery or showcase of completed projects, including descriptions, tools used, and outcomes.
  • Contact Page: Contact information and possibly a simple form for direct communication.

Scenarios

  • What kind of projects has this person worked on before?
  • How can I get in touch to hire them for a project?

Color Schema

The color scheme is designed to be professional but friendly:

  • Primary (Dark Blue): Used for headings and header background.
  • Secondary (Light Gray): Used for body background and section accents.

Typography

  • Heading Font: Montserrat (for headers and titles)
  • Body Font: Open Sans (for paragraphs and general text)
